There were plenty of superlatives and milestones in the history of the G-Class. With the new Mercedes-Maybach G 650 Landaulet, the off-road classic proves that the definition of unique luxury can always be increased. What's more, it keeps reinventing itself and shows once again what potential the off-road classic, which has been in production since 1979, still has today. With a superior V12 engine, portal axles, electric soft top and exclusive equipment in the rear compartment, the very special off-road vehicle, limited to 99 vehicles, meets the expectations of customers who place the highest demands on their vehicle. After the Mercedes-Maybach S-Class and the S 650 Cabriolet, the open-top G-Class is the first off-roader from the Mercedes-Maybach sub-brand. A length of 5,345 millimeters, 3,428 millimeters wheelbase, a height of 2. 235 millimeters, almost half a meter of ground clearance and plenty of space and comfort for four passengers: the G 650 Landaulet surpasses all standards. This extroverted G-Class thus represents a hitherto unique combination of luxurious chauffeur-driven sedan and off-roader. While the driver and front passenger sit under the closed roof, the rear passengers enjoy maximum comfort: at the push of a button, a large folding roof opens electrically and reveals a view of the blue sky. The rear passengers experience the majestic open-air experience on a single seat system. If desired, an electrically operated partition separates the rear from the driver's compartment, and the glass pane changes from transparent to opaque at the touch of a button. Thanks to the wheelbase, which has been extended by 578 millimetres, the passengers in the Mercedes-Maybach G 650 Landaulet can enjoy great freedom of movement. In combination with the first-class rear seats from the S-Class, the seating and space comfort is significantly increased. The active multi-contour seats with ENERGIZING massage function ensure a high level of seating comfort and excellent lateral support thanks to inflatable air chambers. The massage programs increase well-being – based on the principle of a relaxing hot stone massage, also with heat. A large business console with thermal cup holders is integrated between the individual seats so that the rear passengers can enjoy the best possible comfort. They allow drinks to be cooled or heated over a longer period of time. The controls for opening or closing the partition are integrated behind the cup holders. The luxurious, exquisite character of the Landaulet is supported by the high-quality designo upholstery with diamond quilting; it is always designed in two colors and is available in four equipment colors. The customer can choose between three different colors for the soft top and four designo paint colors to match. Exclusive "LANDAULET" lettering is worked into the rim flange of the ceramic-polished, 22-inch light-alloy wheels in a 5-twin-spoke design. Electrically extendable boarding aids facilitate access to the vehicle. A skid plate front and rear, large dimensioned fender extensions in real carbon as well as the spare wheel mounted on the right at the rear with an integrated holder for the third brake light are further exclusive design elements. As is known from the off-road icon, the "G" is always breaking new ground: unlike previous landaulets, which are based on representative limousines, the Mercedes-Maybach G 650 Landaulet enables unforgettable automotive experiences even off the beaten track. The portal axles familiar from the G 63 AMG 6x6 and G 500 4x42 create ample ground clearance of 450 millimeters, enabling the open-top off-road vehicle to master even extreme tasks with ease. The most powerful engine available ensures a high level of drive comfort.
