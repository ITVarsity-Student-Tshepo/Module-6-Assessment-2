MERCEDES-MAYBACH S650 CABRIOLET








  • New car with factory mileage
  • One of only 300 S650 Maybach Cabriolets built
  • Incl. new service and TÜV
Construction year: 2017
Paintwork: 040U Black
Interior: Leather Brown
Transmission: Automatic
Milage: 65 KM
Power: 630 hp
Fuel: Petrol
Price: €309 400

The Mercedes-Maybach S 650 Cabriolet is based on the open-top S-Class and has, among other things, adopted the classic aesthetic proportions from it, the highlights of which include the dynamic silhouette with the coupé-like hood and the dropping line of the side wall sloping backwards. One of the special features is the high-end equipment in the interior. The drive is provided by the 6.0 liter V12 with an output of 463 kW (630 hp). There will be 300 examples of this open four-seater worldwide. Each Mercedes-Maybach Cabriolet comes with a car cover made of high-quality fabric in the same color as the hood, with diamond quilting and an embroidered Maybach emblem and lettering on the bonnet. Exclusive Mercedes-Maybach features include a new front bumper with an upgraded lower section and numerous chrome accents to visibly emphasize the special claim to luxury. The Maybach emblem is unobtrusively but elegantly staged on the fender. Another special feature on the exterior are the 20-inch Maybach forged wheels in two-tone design. When the doors are opened, the Maybach logo is projected onto the road using the entry lighting. In the interior, the fully leather-covered Mercedes-Maybach S 650 Cabriolet implements some of the visionary ideas of the Concept S-Class Coupé show car (IAA 2013) in series production. This includes the dynamic "flow of lines" in the seats. This waterfall-like graphic is realized using progressive perforation. The diamond quilting on the outside of the seat bolsters and the Budapest-style piping also testify to the attention to detail. All of the special equipment of the S-Class Cabriolet is standard on board the Mercedes-Maybach, including AIRCAP, AIRSCARF and Swarovski headlights. The 6.0 liter biturbo V12 with an output of 463 kW (630 hp) guarantees a sovereign drive of the luxury convertible. The 7G-TRONIC is responsible for power transmission to the rear axle. The AIRMATIC chassis with stepless damping control ensures a special driving experience. The first cabriolet from the Mercedes-Maybach brand is making its debut at the LA Auto Show and will be on the market in spring 2017 – limited to 300 units at a net price of 300,000 euros.
