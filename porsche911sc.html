With the 911 Sport Classic, Porsche is once again reviving the tradition of exclusive small series in limited numbers. The special character of this 911 is the double-dome roof, reminiscent of the 911 Panamericana study from 1989 and the Carrera GT super sports car presented in 2000. But that's not all, another legendary Porsche model is quoted: the 911 Carrera RS 2.7 from 1972. Porsche advertised its top model at the time with the slogan "Only 500 men will drive it". The most striking feature of the 210 hp lightweight construction were a plastic front spoiler and the trunk lid with the part that went down in automotive history as the "ducktail" and that our Sport Classic also wears again. The Carrera RS hit like a bomb at the time and Zuffenhausen sold more than the originally planned 500 units, namely 1,525. Something like that will not happen with the 911 Sport Classic: There were definitely only 250 copies of it. The 911 Sport Classic is based on the rear-wheel drive Carrera S, but has a 44 millimeter wider rear end with an increased rear track width. At the front there is a "SportDesign" front apron with its own spoiler lip. At the rear, the already mentioned fixed rump rounds off the special appearance. The car has the well-known 3.8-liter six-cylinder with direct fuel injection. However, the performance has been increased by 23 to 408 hp This was ensured by a revision of the intake tract, which optimized performance and torque, especially in the medium speed range, as well as modified cylinder heads, a new suction system and a new engine control. The Porsche engineers proudly refer to the exclusive variable resonance intake system, in which six vacuum-controlled flaps switch between performance and torque-optimized geometry. They are opened or closed simultaneously depending on engine speed and accelerator pedal position. The result: an optimized vibration of the air in the intake tract, which ensures improved filling of the combustion chamber. An exhaust system specially developed for the vehicle ensures the right exhaust back pressure, and with the high-gloss tailpipes including mesh insert and the upper shell of the air filter housing in real carbon, the demands for a performance-oriented look are also taken into account. The Sport Classic accelerates from zero to 100 with 4, 6 seconds, a tenth of a second faster than the Carrera S. The 911 Sport Classic is only available with a six-speed manual gearbox. Ceramic brakes ensure first-class deceleration. The PASM sports suspension including lowering by 20 millimeters and a mechanical rear differential lock together with the wide track ensure a breathtaking driving experience. Porsche has put a lot of effort into the interior design of the 911 Sport Classic. The dashboard is covered with leather in the color "espresso". This forms an attractive contrast to the exterior paint finish in "sportclassic gray" with dark decorative stripes that are exclusive to all 250 vehicles. Braided leather has seldom become beautiful. It is woven from strips of leather and yarn and is used in the center sections of the sports seats and in the door panels. The legendary Fuchsfelge is still known from the 1960s: black with a silver rim, designed by Porsche and produced by Fuchs. The 19-inch Sport Classic wheel with a high-gloss black painted rim star is a new interpretation and successfully rounds off the car's visual appearance.

The vehicle offered here was delivered by the Porsche Center in Stuttgart and is in absolute new condition with a mileage of only 500 kilometers. We are handing over this Porsche 911 Sport Classic to its future owner, including customer service at the Porsche Center in Zuffenhausen.
